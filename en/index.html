1. General Information

This Privacy Policy applies to the Android app German–Macedonian Dictionary – DiTu (hereinafter "App"). We take the protection of your personal data seriously and treat your personal data confidentially and in accordance with applicable data protection laws and this Privacy Policy.

2. Data Collected

The App collects and processes the following data:

  • Usage data: Anonymous statistics about App usage
  • Device information: Operating system version, device type
  • Crash reports: Technical error reports to improve the App
  • Account data (optional): Email address and encrypted password when creating a user account

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of the App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect the following data:

  • Advertising ID of the device
  • IP address
  • Location data (if permitted)

For more information, please refer to the Google Privacy Policy.

Advertising can be permanently disabled by purchasing the Premium version.

4. Crash Reports (Sentry)

The App uses Sentry to capture crash reports. Technical information such as error messages and device information is transmitted, but no personal data. For more information: Sentry Privacy Policy.

5. User Account and Database Service (Supabase)

The App optionally allows you to create a user account. Registration and management of user accounts is handled via Supabase (Supabase Inc., 65 Chulia Street #38-02/03, OCBC Centre, Singapore 049513).

When creating a user account, the following personal data is collected and stored:

  • Email address
  • Password (stored encrypted; no access in plain text)
  • Account creation date and time

This data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Authentication and access to your user account
  • Synchronisation of your personal App settings
  • Restoring access when switching devices

Legal basis: Processing is carried out on the basis of Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of a contract) and Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent).

Data storage: Your data is stored on Supabase servers. Supabase uses AWS data centres. The transfer of data to third countries (USA) is carried out on the basis of the EU Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses.

For more information on data processing by Supabase: Supabase Privacy Policy.

6. Deletion of User Account

You can delete your account directly in the App under Settings → Account → Delete Account.

Alternatively, send an email to: tumbov.software@gmail.com. In this case, all stored personal data will be deleted within 30 days.

Voluntary: Creating a user account is entirely optional. The core features of the App (dictionary search) are available without an account.

7. In-App Purchases

The App offers in-app purchases via Google Play. Payment processing is handled exclusively by Google Play. We have no access to your payment data. For more information: Google Play Privacy.

8. Offline Use

The Premium version of the App enables full offline use. In this case, no data is transmitted to external servers.

9. Sharing of Data with Third Parties

We do not share your data with third parties, except with the services mentioned in this Privacy Policy (Google AdMob, Sentry, Supabase, Google Play).
